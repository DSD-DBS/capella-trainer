## What is Capella?

Capella is an open-source model-based systems engineering tool that provides comprehensive support for the design of complex systems. Developed by Thales, Capella is based on the ARCADIA method and offers a rich set of features for modeling, analysis, and documentation.

## Understanding MBSE

Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) is a methodology that uses models to support the full lifecycle of system development. MBSE enhances the ability to manage complexity, improve communication among stakeholders, and ensure consistency and traceability throughout the development process.

## The ARCADIA Method

ARCADIA (Architecture Analysis and Design Integrated Approach) is a model-based engineering method developed by Thales. It provides a structured approach to system design, focusing on architecture and ensuring that all stakeholder needs are addressed. The ARCADIA method is fully supported by the Capella tool.
